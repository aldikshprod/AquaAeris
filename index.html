AquaAeris

Your source for sustainable hydration

Our Solution

AquaAeris is a self-contained device that generates drinking water from air using the environmentally friendly physical properties of the Peltier element, thus solving one of the most important environmental problems

Problem

Shortage of drinking water:

25% of the world's population suffers from water shortages

2 million people die every year from water shortages

In the villages of Kazakhstan only 57.9% of households have central water supply

3.6 billion people consume contaminated water.

Secondary problems:

  • Inefficient water management
  • Water scarcity in agriculture and livestock
  • Climate change and natural disasters

How it works

Advantages of AquaAeris Technology

Unique and Innovative
AquaAeris system extracts water from the humidity in the air, an unlimited source of water. The air is a safer, cleaner platform from which to extract water, compared to soil.
Premium Drinking Water Quality
AquaAeris heat-exchange product complies with all Food Grade standards.
New Water Source
AquaAeris products create a completely new drinking water source from the air around us.
Scalable Structure
AquaAeris versions can be configured to any size and supports a wide range of water production needs.
Energy Efficient & Cost-Effective
AquaAeris produce 1 liter of clean drinking water using as little as 0.15 KWH in a wide range of climatic conditions, in temperatures ranging from -15 and 40℃ and humidity ≥20%RH.
Eco-friendly
Our products are environmentally friendly and do not use freon gases that pollute the atmosphere.
Global Industry Recognition and Certification
AquaAeris technology is fully compliant with American ASSE LEC 2004 standards. In addition, our products comply with EPA, NSF61 and federal drinking water standards.
Production at Point of Use
No water transportation is needed to drinking water on tap. Pure, tasty water is produced right from the air, no matter how badly polluted the environment, how rusty the water pipes, or how bad the condition of the local infrastructure is.

IoT - Technology

The solar panel rotates 360 depending on the data from the photoresistor, directing it with the help of a servo drive to the side with maximum solar luminosity

Data from temperature and humidity sensors, water sensor, and water chemical analysis sensor are transmitted via WIFI module to the application in real time.

As well as reducing the lead time between order and delivery, mass production also ensures the reliability of our technology.
